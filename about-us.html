About Us

Who are we?

We are the Food & Bioproduct Sciences Club at University of Saskatchewan! where our passion for all things food unites us! We are a vibrant community of students who share an interest in food industries and a keen interest in studying and exploring the fascinating world of food.

Membership

What is our goal?

FABS club strives to create a welcoming environment where students can share interests and form connections. With our diverse range of events and opportunities for personal and professional development, the FABS club creates memories in your university journey that will last a lifetime.
